Welcome to Hazelnut Books!

At Hazlenut Books, we believe every child has a story to tell. As one of India's pioneering self-publishing houses, we provide a platform for young authors to showcase their talent and creativity. From poems to stories, haiku to travelogues, our mission is to empower children and youth to share their voices through literature.

Why Hazelnut?

• Exclusive Youth Focus: Hazlenut Books specializes in publishing works created by children and youth, celebrating their unique perspectives and narratives. We also welcome contributions from adults, especially parents and grandparents, to share their stories for children.
• Connecting to Your Roots: We reach out to the diaspora in various countries, publishing success stories and experiences with children in their home countries to strengthen familial bonds.
• Diverse Formats: We offer books in multiple formats including print, audio, e-book, flipbooks, braille, and more, ensuring accessibility for all readers.
• Customized Packages: Our flexible publishing packages are tailored to meet the specific needs and preferences of our customers, whether they are individuals or institutions.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to fostering a love for reading and writing among children. In an age dominated by screens, we aim to revive the joy of reading with engaging and meaningful content crafted by young minds. Our vision is encapsulated in "one child one book". ensuring every child has access to books that inspire and educate.
